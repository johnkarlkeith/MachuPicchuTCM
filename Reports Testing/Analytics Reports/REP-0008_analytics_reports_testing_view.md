## **REP-0007:** Analytics Reports testing - View  

> **Summary:** Verify that analytics is viewable in dashboard successfully.  <br>

**Preconditions:**

 - User is logged in.
 - At least one transaction and product record exists in the system.

Scenario 1 

 | \# | Step | Expected Behavior | 
 |----|------|-------------------| 
 |  1 | Navigate to the "Dashboard" page.                                   | Dashboard loads successfully with visual components.   | 
 |  2 | Observe the analytics section (charts, summaries, figures).         | Key statistics such as income, revenue, sales, profit, and etc.  | 

**Post-conditions:**  

 - User can view real-time analytics and summaries. 
 - Visual elements (charts, figures) dynamically update as data changes.
